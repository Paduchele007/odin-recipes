Chicken Breasts with Chipotle Green Onion Gravy

This delicious chicken recipe includes a simple exercise in pan gravy perfection.

If I only had one day to teach someone how to cook, you better believe this archetypal sauce recipe would be one of the first things I'd demonstrate.

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • salt and pepper
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • flour

Steps

  1. Pound chicken
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Cook in skillet for 5 minutes each side
  4. Add green onion and chili powder
  5. Cook 2 minutes more in skillet
