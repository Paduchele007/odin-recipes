French Toast

There are many, fancy variations on this basic recipe.

This easy recipe works with many types of breadâ€”white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French.

Ingredients

  • white bread
  • eggs
  • milk
  • vanilla and cinnamon
  • salt

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, both sides.
