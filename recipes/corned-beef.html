Corned Beef

A combination of corned beef, potatoes, and onions.

A quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Can of Corned Beef
  • Onion
  • Beef Broth

Steps

  1. Add potatoes beef and onion in skillet
  2. Cover and simmer unitll potatoes are of mashing consistency
  3. Mix well and serve
